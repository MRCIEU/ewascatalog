Upload

If you would like to have your EWAS added to the The EWAS Catalog, you can simply fill out an online form to describe each EWAS in your publication (the meta-data) and upload corresponding summary statistics. To access the meta-data form and upload your summary statistics, please email The EWAS Catalog team at ewascatalog@outlook.com.

Through this upload process, you also have the option to make your full summary statistics available, and citable, via Zenodo. This can be indicated on the meta-data form. The EWAS Catalog team will sort this out and send you the DOI corresponding to your dataset. Click here to be taken to an example of a dataset uploaded to Zenodo via The EWAS Catalog pipeline.
